## Tips

- Send 10-15 cold emails per day. Consistency beats volume.
- Personalize the first line. Mention something real about their business.
- Follow up once. 70% of responses come from follow-ups.
- Track opens/clicks with a free tool like Mailtrack (Gmail) or Hunter.io
- Once you get 1-2 clients, replace the testimonial placeholders on your site with real quotes.
