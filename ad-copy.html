Ad Testing Strategy

  • Week 1: Launch 3 ad variants with $20/day each
  • Week 2: Kill the worst performer. Double budget on the winner.
  • Week 3: Test new headline/CTA on the winning creative
  • Always: Track cost per acquisition (CPA) — aim for 3x return on ad spend (ROAS)
  • Retargeting: Create a separate campaign for people who visited but didn't buy (lower cost, higher conversion)
